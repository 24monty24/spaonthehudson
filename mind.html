Clear Your Mind

Meditation

Meditation: While there a many ways to meditate, Spa on the Hudson focuses on three methods that we have found to be the most beneficial for ourselves and our clients. All three methods are helpful in reducing stress, calming the mind and bringing about a sense of peace and well-being. We recommend a daily practice! We cannot emphasize this strongly enough! In addition to the sessions at the spa, if you bring meditation into your home for even 5 or 10 minutes a day, you will begin to build a foundation that turns into a positive habit. Little steps and repetition, as the waves build upon themselves on the beach. This is what turns the tide.
