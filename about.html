We aspire to create a welcoming space in which our clients can find refuge from the fast and often times chaotic pace of the city. We hope to guide people to make a commitment to healthy living and to provide them with the tools they need to feel whole and at peace. Our therapies are integrative, a blend of western methodologies, and alternative eastern techniques inspired by worldwide civiliaztions and cultures. It is our goal to treat the whole person. By combining practices from the east and west and drawing on ancient traditions inclucing ayurvedic principals and traditional chinese medicines, we believe we can attain a state of WHOLE WELLNESS.

But the real secret to lifelong good health is actually the opposite: Let your body take care of you.
